Why Choose Us?

At SAI TIRUMALA DIGITALS our main aim is to make it "ONE STOP DESTINATION FOR ALL PRINT NEEDS" keeping in mind Customer Satisfaction with Best Quality Outputs and Service.

Our Areas of Expertise include:

  • UV FLATBED Printing on any flat surfaces such as: Foam Sheet, Flute Board, Glass, Acryllic, Granite, Wood etc.
  • LED Signage Boards, ACRYLIC Signs, NEON Signs, ACP Signs, OPEN LED Signs etc.
  • Laser Cutting(8'x4'), CNC Cutting (8'x4'), Plotter Cutting (Vinyl Sticker Cutting).
  • Flex Printing on Normal Flex, Star Flex, Blockout etc.
  • Solvent and Eco-Solvent Printing on ROLLAND & HP LATEX Machines.
  • Glowsign Boards on Backlit and Vinyl Stickers etc.
  • Multi Color Offset and Digital Color Printing of Visiting Cards, Brochures, Posters etc.

UV Flatbed Printing

Printing on UV FLATBED Machine where you can print on any flat surfaces such as: Foam Sheet, Flute Board, Glass, Acryllic, Granite, Wood etc.

Led Signages

LED Signage Boards, ACRYLIC, NEON, ACP, OPEN LED Signs etc. Laser & CNC Cutting (8'x4'), Plotter Cutting (Vinyl Sticker Cutting).

Flex Printing

Ad Banners, Political Banners Printing on Normal Flex, Star Flex, Block out etc. with frames without frames.

Solvent & Eco Solvent Printing

Solvent & Eco Solvent Printing on Rolland & HP LATEX Machines. Lamination and Pasting on 3mm and 5mm Foam Sheets also available.

Glow Sign Boards

Quality and Durable Sign Boards prepared with BACKLIT and VINYL STICKERING with Best Quality Lights, Framing and Finishing.

Multicolor Offset & Digital Xerox

Printing of Publicity and Promotional Material: Visiting Cards, Letter Heads, Stickers, Leaflets, Brochures, Posters, Calendars, MENUs etc.
